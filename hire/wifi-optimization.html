What I actually fix

Most WiFi complaints do not come from “too little internet”. They come from bad planning and wrong settings.

  • Channel plan: correct channels in 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz (no overlapping chaos)
  • Bandwidths: right widths per radio for stability instead of guessing
  • Transmit power: APs tuned so they cooperate instead of fighting
  • AP placement: review of current placement, clear suggestions where needed
  • SSID & login: guest / staff / IoT layout, captive portal / login flow sanity check

Not included (clear & fair)

  • Re-cabling or construction work
  • Controller migrations or license procurement
  • Replacing defective hardware
Outcome: stable, predictable WiFi with fewer complaints and clearer visibility of issues.

Why this works

Overlapping channels, wrong power levels and oversized bandwidths overload the air — not your internet line. By cleaning up the RF plan, guests finally get a stable connection in their rooms.
