What I do for you (done-for-you)

  • I set correct channels in 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz (no overlap)
  • I choose proper bandwidths per radio (stable instead of "auto")
  • I adjust transmit power so APs do not fight each other
  • I review AP locations & outline meaningful adjustments
  • SSID layout (guest / staff / IoT) with sensible defaults

Not included (clear & fair)

  • Re-cabling / construction work
  • Controller migrations or license procurement
  • Replacement of defective hardware
Outcome: working WiFi — stable & reliable.

Why optimization works

Overlapping channels and overly wide bandwidths slow networks down. A clean plan decouples APs and delivers consistent performance.
