04 · Action Plan

Sample-Order Checklist

Five non-negotiable steps before committing to any MOQ.

1

Order 5–10 samples per RH level per supplier

Never commit to MOQ before hands-on testing. Request 58%, 62%, 69% for Supplier B; 62%, 65%, 69%, 72% for Supplier A.

2

Verify Alibaba 'Verified Supplier' badge & on-time delivery rate

Check directly on Alibaba. Look for Gold Supplier status and ≥95% on-time delivery. Screenshot for records before proceeding.

3

⚠️ Demand MSDS / full ingredient disclosure — no docs = no deal

A Material Safety Data Sheet is non-negotiable for any branded product. No documentation is a hard red flag. Do not proceed without it.

4

Run a 30-day parallel test vs. Boveda (same RH, calibrated hygrometer)

Log RH deviation daily. Test both Alibaba suppliers against Boveda at identical RH levels and conditions. Document everything.

5

Pay via Alibaba Trade Assurance only — choose Supplier B

Never pay outside the platform. Trade Assurance protects your deposit. Supplier B is the preferred pick: broader RH range (incl. 58%) and stronger private-label depth.

05 · Deep-Dives

Extended Supplier Notes

Expand each panel for detailed analysis and product-specific guidance.

Supplier A — Alibaba +

Format: 8 g flat card-type pouch, similar form factor to Boveda travel size. Suitable for up to ~5 cigars in a travel case.

Chemistry: Likely NaCl/KCl in plant-fibre substrate. No published test data. In high-humidity climates (e.g. Thailand) expect active life closer to 30 days rather than 60.

Supplier type: Typical trading-company listing. Verify Gold/Verified Supplier status directly on Alibaba before any order.

OEM: Custom packaging available; sample cost typically recoverable at MOQ. Suitable for budget inserts where precision is secondary.

Supplier B — Preferred ★ +

Sizes: 4 g for shipping inserts only (~2–4 weeks). 8 g for travel humidors (~30–60 days). The 4 g is not suited for long-term storage.

58% RH: Rare among Chinese OEMs — indicates deeper formulation capability, likely K-acetate or MgCl blend. Makes this the strongest private-label candidate.

Certification caveats: Same concerns as Supplier A. MSDS request is mandatory before any production order.

MOQ note: MOQ for 58% RH may be higher than standard 62–69% levels. Confirm with supplier before ordering samples.

Boveda — Quality Benchmark +

Invented 1997. Patented RO membrane emits only pure water vapour — no liquid, no salt residue on cigars or humidor surfaces. Zero maintenance required.

Trusted globally: Used by the National Gallery of Art to calibrate hygrometers. 3M+ users across cigars, cannabis, musical instruments, and food packaging.

OEM: Not available for private-label. Reference benchmark only — not a sourcing option.

Cost: 5–15× higher unit cost than Alibaba alternatives — offset by longer active life (60–90 vs 30–60 days), certified ±1–2% precision, and full food-safe certification.
